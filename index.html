False Position Numerical Solver (High Precision Mode)

The Regula Falsi method approximates real roots of continuous functions by taking an interval [a, b] where the function changes sign. It finds the intersection point c of the secant line crossing (a, f(a)) and (b, f(b)), systematically narrowing down the bounds until it achieves your requested target precision.
